Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6 Years)

A kindergarten is a special place for children aged 3 to 5 years. It is where young kids learn through fun activities like singing, drawing, and playing. Teachers help children make friends and understand basic concepts like numbers and letters.

Elementary School

An elementary school is where children start formal education, usually from ages 6 to 12. They learn important subjects like math, reading, science, and social studies. Teachers guide students to develop skills, explore creativity, and solve problems.

Read More

Middle (10-16 Years)

Middle school is for students aged 11 to 14, helping them transition from childhood to adolescence. They study advanced subjects like math, science, history, and literature. Middle school encourages independence, critical thinking, and teamwork.
